Frequently asked questions

Good to know before you start.

Planora is actively being developed by DesignLab Creative Studio.

Is Planora free?

Planora is currently available as a free public beta. Paid or premium features may be introduced later, with clear notice before any billing is required.

Does Planora publish directly to social media?

Not yet. The current beta is a planning and workflow tool. It helps you organize content, schedules, captions, and status—not automatically publish to social networks.

Can I use it for several brands?

You can organize social pages within your workspace. Expanded multi-brand limits and team controls are planned for Premium.

Is my planner private?

Planora uses account-based access and database row-level security so users can only access records permitted for their account and workspace.

Can features change during the beta?

Yes. Beta features, limits, and interface details may change as the product is tested and improved. Important updates will appear in the in-app changelog.
